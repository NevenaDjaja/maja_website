City College of San Francisco is Open

City College of San Francisco wanted to correct misconceptions that it might be closing, and refocus public opinion on what the college provides for the community.

By repeatedly emphasizing the word “open”, the card not only informs the reader of the educational opportunities available at City College, but also creates an emotional appeal. Setting the text at a dynamic angle in a variety of colors helps to attract the reader’s attention and strengthens this emotional appeal.
